About My Expertise

Hey, Im Ajoy A G but in most hacker corners of the internet, youll know me as 0xprowl3r.

Im actively carving out my path in Red Teaming and Security Research, with a strong focus on real-world exploitation, CTFs, and bug bounty methodology. My passion lies at the intersection of learning, breaking, and building and I thrive in environments where I can put knowledge into action.
